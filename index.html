William Diebel, PhD

Hi! I'm Will, a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the Department of Management Science at the University of South Carolina - Darla Moore School of Business. My research focuses on the intersection of supply chains and critical environmental issues, with a particular interest in corporate environmental transparency.

Prior to joining USC Moore, I earned my PhD in Operations Management from the Ivey Business School at Western University, where I was a recipient of the Joseph-Armand Bombardier Canada Graduate Scholarship - Doctoral, supported by SSHRC.

My research has been published in the Journal of Operations Management (DOI: 10.1002/joom.1293).

I currently teach the MGSC 395 Operations Management undergraduate degree course in Fall semesters.
